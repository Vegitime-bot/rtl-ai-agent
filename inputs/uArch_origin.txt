# Micro-architecture (origin TCON)
- Well-known IP: LCD Timing Controller driving 1080p60 RGB panel (similar to mainstream display driver ICs).
- Fixed porch/sync parameters baked as module parameters.
- Single set of counters (H,V) feeding HSYNC/VSYNC/DE combinational logic.
- No partial update; entire frame is always scanned out.
- No feedback from panel TE (tearing effect) pin; frame pacing is free-running.
