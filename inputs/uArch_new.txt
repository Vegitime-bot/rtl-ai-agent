# Micro-architecture (new TCON+)
- Target: Display driver IC with ROI partial-update + panel TE handshake (common in AMOLED controllers).
- Adds APB shadow registers for porch and ROI; double-buffer commits at frame boundary.
- ROI gate drives DE only within (x0,x1)/(y0,y1); outside region filled with idle color fetched from LUT.
- Fractional line-timer (16-step accumulator) keeps dot clock aligned to MIPI DSI ESC clock tolerance (<±50 ppm).
- Optional TE synchronizer stretches EOF until TE rising edge, ensuring tear-free refresh.
